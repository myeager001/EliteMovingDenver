Frequently. Asked. Questions.

How long has Elite Movers Denver been in business?
We started in 2016.

How do I know I’ve chosen a reputable company?
Elite Movers Denver has a 5 star reputation with Thumbtack, Yelp, Google, We are the favored mover by real estate agents throughout the Denver metro area.

Is my move insured?
Free FULL COVERAGE ON ALL LOCAL MOVES. Elite Movers Denver offers full replacement value coverage as part of every local move at no additional charge. This covers you against any loss or damage on items we pack and move. (see terms and conditions)

Is there anything you won’t move?
We can’t move anything that is flammable, corrosive or explosive. In other words, we will be happy to move your BBQ grill, but please make separate arrangements for your propane tank. Please empty gas tanks. Please take cleaning fluids yourself or leave them behind. Please dispose of all opened food items before the move.

Do the movers take a lunch break?
Yes. Our movers do take lunch breaks during the working day, and you will not be charged for it.

Do you move pianos?
Yes. Elite Movers Denver has movers that are specially trained in moving pianos. Our trucks are equipped with the special equipment needed to move pianos. Whether your piano is a standard upright or a Steinway Concert Grand, it is in very good hands with Elite Movers.

What time will my movers arrive?
We will arrive on time during the ETA window in your confirmation letter. If you have chosen an afternoon time slot, we will arrive as soon as possible after completing a morning job.

Do I have to empty the file cabinets?
Metal vertical file cabinets can remain full, but lateral and wooden files must be emptied. The drawer slides on lateral and wood files are not made to take the weight of the files during moving per the manufacturers.

Do I have to empty the freezer or refrigerator?
Yes, the chest freezers can remain full. Refrigerators must be emptied to avoid the contents damaging the inside of your appliance.

Will Elite Movers Denver remove the mirror from the dresser?
Yes. Elite Movers will bring along all the necessary tools that are needed to remove and re-install any mirrors attached to your dresser. Items mounted to the wall will require 3rd party services. These include: hung pictures, mirrors, three dimensional art pieces and shelves.

How big are your trucks?
We own and operate our own moving vans.

Will Elite Movers Denver take apart my beds?
Yes. Elite Movers will bring all the tools to disassemble and reassemble your beds. If your bed has air or water fill, please empty it before moving day. Some beds do require additional services and costs, such as: log beds, very complicated bunk bed systems and canopy beds. All the bedding should be removed before the movers arrive.

Do you provide packing services?
Yes. Our fully trained packing and crating staff is ready to help you with any size project. We also have professional organizers available to help with the pre-packing sorting and organizing.

Do you offer storage?
No, at this time we do not offer any storage.

What do you consider a large box and what do you consider a small box?
Large boxes are to be carried one at a time. Small boxes can be carried two or more at a time.

Can I leave clothing in my dressers?
Yes. Elite Movers will move your dressers with the drawers in them. In very tight hallway situations, it's better to have the drawers packed. Also antique, particle board and generally weak furniture should be completely empty. You can leave cloth items in the drawers. Please remove all jewelry, perfume and non-clothing items.

Do you move out of state?
Yes. Elite Movers Denver is happy to accommodate all moves. We are fully licensed and prepared to move you anywhere.

How do you protect my house?
Elite Movers carry a number of items to protect your new and existing home from damage. We have floor runners to protect your carpets and floors, doorway protectors for your front door and pads for cabinets and railings. We will also use our moving pads for additional protection as needed.

What type of payments do you accept and when?
We accept cash, personal checks, MasterCard, Visa and Discover.

Do movers get tips?
Although it’s not required, many of our customers have shown their appreciation by tipping the movers.

Do you unhook appliances?
Yes, we are happy to unhook gas or water lines on appliances. We do recommend that you replace your old water line hoses on your washer when you move. These can be purchased at most hardware stores.

Do you unhook electronics?
Due to the complexity of today’s technology, our movers cannot be trained in all the different systems that are available today. Because of this, we ask that all electronics are disconnected before we arrive.

Will you dispose of items?
Yes. We have many options to recycle and donate old furniture and moving refuse.

How can I move my hanging clothes?
Elite Movers Denver will move all the contents of your closest as long as they as packed, hanging cloths can be transported in moving pads.

Will you move a piece of furniture within my home?
Yes. Elite Movers Denver can move items within your home. Please ask about pricing.

What happens if an item will not fit in my house?
There are times when an item will not fit at a new home. We will do everything in our power to make the item fit, but we will not disassemble the furniture or your home to make the item fit. If it does not fit, we will place the item wherever you request, or we can take it to storage.

On those larger moves, how much do you charge per piece?
We are very proud to say we do not charge per piece! Elite Movers Denver is an Hourly moving service. Our professionals are experienced in all types of commercial/residential relocation. So whether you want us to move a 3 drawer desser or 1000lb safe, we will only charge you for the time it takes to get the job done.
